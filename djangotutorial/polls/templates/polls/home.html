Polls Application: Full Navigation Guide

App Structure & URLs

This application is divided into three main areas:

  • The Landing Page (/): This is where you are now. It serves as your "How-To" guide and a quick list of active polls.
  • The Polls Index (/polls/): The dedicated app view that lists all questions. You can navigate here directly to see the full list of polls.
  • The Admin Dashboard (/admin/): The secure area for managing content.

User Flow

  1. Navigate to the Polls Index at /polls/ or use the list below.
  2. Click on a question to go to its Detail page (e.g., /polls/1/).
  3. Select a choice and click Vote.
  4. View the final standings on the Results page (e.g., /polls/1/results/).
